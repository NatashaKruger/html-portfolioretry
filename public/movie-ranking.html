The Best Movies According to Natasha

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Grease

Grease is my all time favorite as it combines a soppy love story with some amazing music.

Thelma the Unicorn

This movie is a favorite for my daughter, she will watch it over and over again. How a little horse turned into a unicorn and became all she ever wanted to be, and at the end she realised being herself was all that she indeed needed.

Cinderella Story

This movie is the ultimate love story, it reflects a lot of emotions throughtout the story that indicated how mean kids can be, but despite it all, the end turns out better than expected.
